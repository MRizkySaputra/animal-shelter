How Our Pack Started

It all started with one stray dog, two crazy animal lovers, and a whole lot of treats. We realized that too many good boys and girls were sleeping on the streets instead of cozy sofas. So, we rolled up our sleeves, built a massive playground, and officially became a full-time hotel for pets in between homes!

Our Paw-sitive Impact

  • 10,000+ Treats Happily Devoured
